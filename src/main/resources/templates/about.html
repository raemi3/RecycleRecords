About Our Business

Recycle Records aims to help pre-loved vinyl find love once again! ANYONE can sign up for free to our service as either a buyer or a seller & browse the best secondhand vinyl Ireland has to offer!

As a seller you will not only make a profit but also help reduce the impact the record industry is having on wax demand for re-issued & new vinyl. Recycle Records will only take 5% of your earnings (products + post) so if you make a sale totaling €50 we only earn €2.50 - unlike our main competitors who would take €4!
